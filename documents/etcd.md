etcd
======================
> 使用etcd v3

- 上传二进制安装包到远程主机，安装文件在：repo/softwares/etcd/
```bash
#mac
etcd-v3.3.18-darwin-amd64.zip
#linux
etcd-v3.4.1-linux-amd64.tar.gz
```

- 解包后进入目录，执行启动命令：
````bash
nohup ./etcd &
````
- etcd的UI管理工具[etcd-manager](https://www.npmjs.com/package/etcd-manager)
- [etcd官网](https://etcd.io/)
